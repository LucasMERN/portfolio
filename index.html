About me

I'm a Full Stack Software Engineer based out of Canyon Lake, Texas!

I started my professional career by self teaching the art of compositing videography in Adobe After Effects by myself. As a result of that, I had the opportunity to start freelancing for various content creators in the gaming industry. During this time I discovered my passion for creating digital art with a focus on the consumer experience but even more so I realized how much I enjoyed solving problems that I did not have answers for prior to encountering them. I became enthralled with the process of learning. Which brings me to my next phase in life...

The egineering field is all about finding innovative solutions to problems that we all face in our daily lives, whether it be at home or work. I decided to transition into engineering the digital space to take advantage of my skills in design and to see if I have what it takes to solve real world problems.

When I'm not developing new ideas in my IDE, you can find me playing FPS games on the competitive level, smoking various meats and cheese in my BBQ pit, or taking care of my growing family of rescued pets.
